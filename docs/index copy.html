Understanding Cities Through Social Media

By analyzing location check-in patterns throughout a city, we discover its hidden structure. Our techniques produce dynamic neighborhood maps, reflecting the every-day experiences of real people.

Using Machine-Learning to Study Cities

By looking at people's checkin patterns at places across the city, we create a mapping of the different dynamic areas that comprise it. Each Livehood tells a different story of the people and places that shape it.

Undertsanding Factors Shaping Neigbhorhoods

Our research hypothesis is that the character of an urban area is defined not just by the the types of places found there, but also by the people that make it part of their daily life.

Re-Imagining the City in the Age of Social Media

Livehoods offer a new way to conceptualize the dynamics, structure, and character of a city by analyzing the social media its residents generate.
